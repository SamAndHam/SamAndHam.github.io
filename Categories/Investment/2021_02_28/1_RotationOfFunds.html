Notes on the rotation of funds 2021.

A compilation of sources from sites like Reddit regarding the changes that the market has just experienced during Feb 2021

Stocks, Market Movement, 2021

A $50 Billion Unwind Fueled Treasuries' Rout. Yahoo

User u/rhetorical_twix on Reddit has noted a massive unwinding of positions in tech stocks and pandemic stocks, forcing institutions and trading professionals to change their portfolios to stocks that are oriented towards value. This rotation of funnds cannot be expected to be orderly and was partially triggered by a massive selling of bonds.
The market is now rejecting the Fed's view of the low interest persisting into the future with 6-7% growth being projected due to the stimulus plans.

In the same post, user u/B0atingAccident makes an opposing stand that the rise in bond yields are to be expected and are a signal of a healthy economic recovery. The user points to the example of 1990's where stocks, tech stocks included, saw a dramatic rise. The current drop was noted to be a correction especially for stocks that have been overvalued.
